# MaMa Zainab - Keyframe Storyboard

## Brand Incorporation - The Legend of Wong & MaMa Zainab

**Basis:** Revised “See, And Say” screenplay  
**Aspect:** 2.39:1 anamorphic  
**Target runtime:** ~3:30  
**Storyboard shots:** 46 keyframes  
**Purpose:** Paste/import into the video project parser and generate cinematic keyframes first, then image-to-video takes.

---

## GLOBAL KEYFRAME STYLE LOCK

**Visual language:** photoreal cinematic film stills, ARRI Alexa 35, anamorphic 2.39:1, shallow depth of field when intimate, wide epic framing when geographic, natural skin texture, film grain, volumetric haze where appropriate.

**Color arcs:**

- **Scene 1:** neon pink/cyan/ink black, storm rain, thriller.
- **Scenes 2–6:** warm Egyptian gold, cream, teal sky, lived-in authenticity.
- **Scene 7:** joyful travel montage, sunlit Egypt, playful but cinematic.
- **Scenes 8–10:** Alexandria Mediterranean warmth, brand greens/yellows, emotional payoff.

**Character locks:**

- **Young Wong / rooftop past:** East-Asian male warrior, younger version of Shang Hong Wong, intense eyes, shaved or close-cropped warrior look, dark textured robe, dangerous silent energy.
- **Older Wong / Egypt chapter:** East-Asian man in his 60s, silver-streaked hair tied back loosely, weathered face, subtle scars, calm dangerous eyes, dark silken warrior robes in dramatic scenes, cream linen suit in public/founder scenes.
- **Mama Zainab:** Egyptian woman in her late 50s, warm kind face, olive skin, soft dark eyes, cream headscarf, simple gold hoop earrings, green-base plaid apron with yellow stripes and white weft, flour-dusted capable hands.
- **ZuZu:** plump photoreal white domestic goose, clean fluffy feathers, amber eyes, vivid orange beak and feet, tiny green plaid ribbon only, never dressed anthropomorphically.

**Universal negative prompt:** no logos, no readable text, no watermarks, no warped faces, no extra fingers, no duplicated characters, no deformed hands, no plastic skin, no AI artifact shimmer, no cartoon, no anime, no 3D CGI render, no low-resolution artifacts.
