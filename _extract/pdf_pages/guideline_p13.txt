The Mama Zeinab pattern system is inspired by the 
brand’s core elements and food identity.
It combines simple icons, geometric shapes, and 
organic motifs to create a cohesive visual language 
that feels warm, familiar, and memorable.
These patterns are designed 
to add character and 
consistency across 
packaging, print materials, 
and digital applications 
without overpowering the 
main brand elements.
