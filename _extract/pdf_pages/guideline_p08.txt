Inspired by Nature. Rooted in Tradition.
The Mama Zainab color palette is inspired by the freshness of vine leaves and 
the warmth of homemade cooking.
Green represents natural ingredients, authenticity, and the rich ﬂavor of freshly 
prepared stuffed vine leaves.
Yellow reﬂects warmth, generosity, and the welcoming spirit of a mother’s 
kitchen.
Together, these colors create a visual identity that feels fresh, comforting, and 
deeply connected to tradition.
