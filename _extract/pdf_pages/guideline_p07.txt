LOGO SPace
The MAMA ZAINAB logo is a symbol of tradition, warmth, 
and homemade authenticity.
To preserve its clarity and impact, a clear space must always 
surround the logo.
This safe area ensures visibility, balance, and brand consis-
tency across all applications.
No text, graphics, or other visual elements should enter this 
protected space.
Maintaining proper spacing strengthens 
recognition and keeps the MAMA ZAINAB 
identity clean, conﬁdent, and timeless.
