## 1. MaMa ZAINAB - *the heart*

### Anchor block (paste into every AI prompt that includes her)

```
Egyptian woman in her late 50s, warm kind face, soft dark eyes, gentle smile lines,
olive skin, headscarf in soft cream tone, wearing a GREEN-base plaid apron with
yellow stripes and white weft (PLAID v2), simple gold hoop earrings, silver wedding
band, flour-dusted hands, dignified posture, calm matriarchal energy.
[REF: 02_Characters/Mama Zainab (Final).jpeg]
```

### Identity card

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Age | 55–60 |
| Build | Slightly stout, grandmotherly, strong forearms |
| Skin | Warm olive |
| Hair | Dark with grey streaks (always covered) |
| Headscarf | Cream / soft yellow / olive - matte cotton, never silk, never patterned |
| Apron | **GREEN-base plaid v2** ([01_Brand/plaid_v2/tile_apron.png](../01_Brand/plaid_v2/tile_apron.png)) - never the legacy yellow-base plaid |
| Jewelry | Single pair of small gold hoops, plain wedding band - nothing else |
| Hands | Flour or herb dust acceptable; never manicured nails |
| Posture | Upright, calm, hands often folded or rolling |
| Expression default | Warm half-smile, eyes crinkled |

### Voice & dialogue rules

- **Voice:** ElevenLabs v3 - warm 50s Egyptian woman, low chest voice, slight rasp.
- **Language:** Egyptian colloquial Arabic first; English with mild Egyptian accent for international cuts.
- **Phrasing:** Short. Wise. Dry humor. Never corporate, never slang.
- **Catchphrases:**
  - «طعم البيت في إيد ماما» - *Home flavor in Mama's hand.*
  - "Tell them no. In this kitchen, we do it the village way, or not at all."
  - «اللي يحبني... ياكل مني» - *Those who love me… eat from me.*

### Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Show her cooking, plating, teaching, laughing | Show her in modern fashion, makeup, or jewelry beyond the rules |
| Use natural sunlight or warm kitchen tungsten | Use neon, club lighting, or hard fluorescents |
| Hands always doing something purposeful | Make her stiff, stoic, or aggressive |
| Plaid apron on green base, exactly | Use the legacy yellow-base apron (legacy assets are reference-only) |
| Headscarf always present | Remove headscarf in any branded surface |
| Center frame, eye contact, golden hour | Distort, glamorize, or sexualize |

### Surface usage

- Logo lockups (alongside wordmark) · packaging hero panel · kiosk poster · website hero · app onboarding · in-store standee · social hero portraits · cinematic film.

## 2. ZUZU - *the goose, the mascot*

### Anchor block

```
Plump healthy white domestic goose, fluffy clean feathers, alert curious posture,
expressive amber eyes, vivid orange beak and feet, wearing a tiny ribbon around
the neck made of GREEN-base plaid v2 fabric (matching Mama Zainab's apron),
slight comedic personality, photoreal feather detail, never anthropomorphic
clothing beyond the ribbon.
[REF: 02_Characters/ZuZu.JPEG]
```

### Identity card

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Species | Domestic white goose (*Anser anser domesticus*) |
| Personality | Mischievous protector - Mama's sidekick & enforcer |
| Costume | **Plaid ribbon only** - no hats, no glasses, no human clothes |
| Eye color | Amber / honey |
| Beak & feet | Vivid orange |
| Sound | Honk used as comic punctuation, never aggressive |
| Power level (lore) | Sees the Ghost of Zainab; trips rivals; flaps flour clouds |

### Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Tiny plaid ribbon around the neck | Aprons, hats, sunglasses, T-shirts |
| Walk-on cameo in every scene she's in | Make ZuZu the focus of food shots (food first, ZuZu garnish) |
| Comedic timing, "judging" looks | Cute baby-style anthropomorphic eyes |
| Use as silhouette in ZuZu icon set | Photoshop a human face onto her |

### Surface usage

- App loading splash · kids menu · loyalty program ("ZuZu Points") · sticker pack · social shorts · cinematic comic relief · ribbon-icon dingbat in print.

### ZuZu-only sub-marks (to design later)

- ZuZu silhouette badge (single color)
- "ZuZu Approved" stamp for combo meals
- ZuZu loyalty stamp card

## 3. SHANG HONG WONG (盛恒王) - *the founder, the legend*

### Anchor block

```
East-Asian man in his 60s, salt-and-pepper hair tied back loosely, weathered face
with old fight scars (subtle), calm dangerous eyes, wearing dark silken warrior
robes in cinematic scenes OR a premium minimalist cream-linen suit in business
scenes, dignified silent presence, slow deliberate movement.
[REF: 02_Characters/WongHong.png]
```

### Two modes

| Mode | When | Costume | Posture |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Warrior** | Scenes 1, 2 (origin/strategy) | Dark silken warrior robes, no logos | Coiled stillness |
| **Banker** | Scenes 3, 6 (plaza, command center) | Cream linen suit, no tie, polished shoes | Calm executive |

### Voice & dialogue rules

- **Voice:** ElevenLabs v3 - Wise Asian Elder, lower pitch 5%, gravel +20%.
- **Languages:** Mandarin (zh-CN) in cinematic scenes; broken English with thick Mandarin accent in plaza/business scenes.
- **Tone:** Spare, weary → tactical → reborn calm.
- **Never:** raise his voice, smile widely, use slang, appear in food shots.

### Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Cinematic, dim lighting, smoke/wind/rain | Show him cooking, plating, or eating |
| Always in shadow or backlit when possible | Put him on packaging or menus |
| Reference 盛恒王 only in lore/credits | Brand him as a co-spokesperson with Zainab |
| Use as "executive producer" mythos | Show him touching Mama Zainab - strict respect distance |

### Surface usage

- Cinematic film only · founder page on website (silhouette + lore) · brand-story PDF for partners · never on packaging · never on menu · never on social food posts.

## 4. GHOST OF ZAINAB - *the mystic*

### Anchor block

```
Translucent younger version of Mama Zainab in her 20s, same warm features but
luminous, pale glowing edges in brand green #1B9B00 at 30% glow, hair softly wind-
blown, wearing the same plaid apron rendered in semi-transparent emerald light,
visible only to Mama Zainab and ZuZu, ethereal heat-haze quality.
[REF: derived - generate with Mama Zainab face, age-regress 30 years, add green
luminescence]
```

### Rules

- **Video only.** Never appears in print, packaging, or social stills.
- Always **partially transparent** (40–60% opacity).
- Glow color is **exactly `#1B9B00`** - no other hue.
- Appears beside Mama Zainab or perched on a windowsill.
- Silent - communicates via gestures and minor weather effects (cold wind, mist).

### Surface usage

- Cinematic film (Scenes 4 & 6) · long-form social cuts only · never standalone.

## 5. Cast group rules

### Hierarchy on any surface

1. **Mama Zainab** - always largest / centered when present.
2. **ZuZu** - supporting, lower-third, never larger than Mama's head.
3. **Wong** - separate frame from food; never beside Mama in still imagery.
4. **Ghost** - only in video, only beside Mama or ZuZu.

### Forbidden combos

- ❌ ZuZu and Wong in the same frame (different worlds - comedy vs. legend).
- ❌ Mama Zainab in any silken or "warrior" wardrobe (preserves character integrity).
- ❌ Any character on the "STOP / We are not Dolma" disruption posters - those are typographic only.

### Color discipline per character

| Character | Allowed dominant colors | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Mama Zainab | green (#1B9B00) plaid + cream + warm sunlight | yellow as accent only |
| ZuZu | white + orange beak/feet + plaid ribbon | no other costume colors |
| Wong | black, charcoal (warrior) **or** cream linen (banker) | never green or yellow on his body |
| Ghost | luminous green (#1B9B00) at 30–50% | nothing else |
